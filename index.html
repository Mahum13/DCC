Ambreen Khalid

When people experience trauma or severe life stresses, it is not uncommon for their lives to unravel. After all, life has to crack open the heart to let the light shine through.

Ambereen’s great passion is to heal by helping align the mind, body, and soul. She realises that this cannot be a quick fix,rather, it is a life long process which needs to be nurtured with compassion.

Being a NLP master coach and a yoga meditation teacher, she helps people find their highest calling and purpose in life by changing the prism with which they look at their lives.

Mother to two amazing adult children and a gorgeous german shepherd, Ambereen’s personal mantra is: Let the heart love,
the mind surrender,
and the soul breathe.

The different roles life presents us can be challenging and sometimes we even get lost in them. I did. Finding the lost part of myself, becoming the creator of my journey by overcoming my negative beliefs and emotions was my journey to awakening. To live a life consciously. A life that served me and people around me.

Through NLP, my learnings, my stories and service as a Coach, I trust I can provide guidance and inspiration to whoever is ready to receive it. To whoever is ready to consciously create and live their life!

With humility and love,
Amina.
