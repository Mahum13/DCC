HOW TO RUN A SUCCESSFUL WORKSHOP

In this 4-day training you will experience amazing transformation as you go from learning the techniques of presenting to becoming an outstanding speaker by day 4, able to stand with confidence and share your message with authenticity and influence and captivating your audience.

You will learn the elegance of the 4Mat system for presenting, learn how to captivate and hold your audience. Learn Ericksonian language and Satir techniques and the use of metaphors to share your message with ease and elegance.

Whether you are an experienced trainer or a novice, this training will assist you to deepen your ability to run workshops that are fun, interesting and most of all impactful for your audience.

At the end of the training there will be a practical assessment and you will receive a certificate of achievement.

Duration: 4-day Training (in-person)

LEADERSHIP COACHING

The leadership coaching workshop will give you the unique skills and knowhow to become a leadership and executive coach and immediately apply your coaching skills in the professional arena. The course focuses on real world and current global contexts and challenges and how to help clients and teams develop their leadership acumen and social/emotional skills.

This course is ideal for coaches who want to specialise in corporate and leadership coaching, as well as professionals, HR managers, business owners and people in leadership roles who are interested in developing and leading a coaching culture in their organisation.

Delivery Method: Hybrid - online & in-person

Duration: 4-days x2 modules course

MINDFUL PARENTING

The first 7 years of a child’s life are critical for their mental, emotional and physical growth. This period has an immense influence on the life they will lead as adults and set the foundations for the beliefs and challenges they face while growing up.

This workshop is an ideal opportunity to facilitate adults to develop a respectful, generous and caring relationship with infants and toddlers.

Mindful parenting will equip all adults working/living with young children with tools, techniques and understandings to build healthy attached relationships and to guide attuned attachment with their children. To support their children, to build strong self-esteem, while they explore their world.

Duration: 2-day workshop (online)

RESPECTFUL PARENTING

The pre-teen and teenage years are some of the most challenging for parents and children and their relationship dynamic.

Respectful parenting equips all adults, either working or living with pre-teen and teenagers, with tools and techniques to be able to better understand your teenager and to be able to communicate better with them and to enhance connection and trust in your relationship dynamic.

The techniques taught at this workshop help you to be a more resourceful parent, which allows for your child to feel safe and builds trust.

Duration: 2-day workshop
