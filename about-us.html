DYNAMIC COMMUNICATIONS

is an Australasian coaching and training company with a truly global reach. We have been working in the space of human excellence and empowering people for over a decade.

Our Students, our clients and our partners love the transformational experience they have through our trainings, our programs and our coaching opportunities.

Our mission is to create excellence through transformation. To empower our students and clients to recognise, embrace and step into their own magnificence, to step into courage and dare to dream and achieve their goals. To aim high and live life on purpose.

Our trainings set the benchmark of excellence that our clients, students and partners associate with working with us, which enables ‘in the room’ transformation we are known for.

“Be the change you want to see”

Our core values that underpin everything we do and that have enabled our success on the global stage, is the difference that makes the difference

  • Live life with passion – we passionately believe in what we do and thus each day, each training, each coaching session is an adventure to be experienced and to be excited by and to bring our best game every single time.

  • Deliver Excellence – We bring our best game to everything we do. Our goal is to WOW our clients and our students. For them to walk away transformed, empowered and happy EVERY SINGLE TIME.

  • Embrace Courage – nothing great has ever been achieved by staying in ones comfort zone. Life is an adventure to be embraced and explored. We dare to push the boundaries, to explore the unknown, which enables our students and clients to do the same. Achieving your dreams lies once you step beyond your comfort zone.
  • Have Fun - We love everything we do and have fun doing it. Life is meant to be enjoyed. When we give ourselves permission to express our own quirky uniqueness we connect into our innate greatness.

  • Take responsibility - Taking responsibility is the cornerstone of success. We own our outcomes – the good, the bad and the indifferent. When we succeed, we celebrate, when we don’t we take stock, own the outcome and find another way to achieve desired outcome. The core value of all successful people is that the buck stops with us. Full ownership means we have the power to create what we desire.

  • Love & connection. - one for all and all for one – we are a family. Your success is our success. Seeing the transformation taking place in the lives of our students and clients is the reward we seek.

  • Innovation - we never settle with the status quo. We are always looking to see how we can improve, adapt and enhance everything we do to add even more value to the experience we offer in all our programs.

  • Insatiable curiosity - even after over a decade, we are still a green and growing company. Our eyes are wide open as there is always so much more to learn and grow. We embrace the value of being lifelong learners.

  • Uniqueness – We believe in standing out and owning our own identity. Thinking outside the box and having the courage and to embrace being thought leaders and trend setters in a competitive environment.

  • Deliver on Results – And putting it together, we take responsibility for what we promise EVERY SINGLE TIME.

Aliyah Mohyeddin

Founder and CEO
Our Founder & CEO Aliyah Mohyeddin is an experienced and internationally recognized Master Trainer of NLP (Neuro-Linguistic Programming), consultant, and facilitator in the field of human behavior and leadership. She is passionate about guiding individuals to build confidence and personal resilience to become high functioning, outstanding leaders in their work, relationships and personal lives.
She brings a fresh, down-to-earth approach focused on overcoming limiting beliefs and roadblocks and maximizing potential, resulting in enhanced levels of success.

Aliyah has over 10 years' experience in training and coaching individuals, teams and organizations, function at their best. She has been an invited trainer in countries such as the Australia, Pakistan, UK, Spain and many more.

NLP is the study of excellence strategies, overcoming limiting beliefs, traumas and other roadblocks holding one back, to achieve personal goals and success in our lives, relationships and work. NLP studies the relationship between language, thoughts and patterns of behaviour as indicators of our subjective outcomes.

Through her coaching and training Aliyah helps individuals in all areas of life enhance their performance and effectiveness in a range of areas including communication, wellbeing, leading others, managing performance, problem solving, engagement, building resilience and developing a mindset for success.
Her passion is in enabling transformation in organizations and people through learning new skills for success in their lives, their relationships and in their careers and education.
