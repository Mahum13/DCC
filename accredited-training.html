This is a blended training with in-the-room and online options available. Experience the training from the comfort of your own home while feeling like you are present in the training room; or attend in-person at our training venue. COVID SOP's are being followed.

This training will help you overcome your limiting beliefs, your negative emotions and those pesky fears, that leave you stuck, frustrated, anxious and depressed. Help you gain back your personal power, create more successful strategies and embrace the best version of yourself in your career, your relationships and life itself.

NLP is the study of the subjective experience of reality; how we construct our perception of the world and our experience of it. NLP is also the modelling of success to get desired outcomes.

Our NLP Practitioner training is a 15-day in-depth immersion into NLP tools and techniques. The training is built to be a transformational personal/professional development experience as well as the necessary pre-requisite for those interested in enrolling in the Master Practitioner training or interested in becoming life coaches.

This course is ideal for:
  • Business leaders, HR Professionals, Salespeople, Managers, Teachers, Parents, Therapists, Doctors.
  • Coaches or people looking to build a career in coaching (the NLP Practitioner and NLP Master Practitioner qualifications are frequently sought after by people looking for life coaching and business coaching).
  • Therapists who wish to compliment their skills with NLP Techniques and Processes.
  • Anyone who wishes to grow, become more successful in their, work, relationships or life itself.
  • Those who want to improve their communication abilities.
  • Those who feel stuck and can't find the path to move forward to achieve greater happiness, fulfilment and success in their lives.
  • Existing NLP Practitioners who wish to revise and refine their NLP skills.

Achieve mastery of NLP by going deeper than ever before, by taking your NLP training to a whole new level.
The NLP Master Practitioner Certification Training deep dives into the NLP patterns you learned in the NLP Practitioner Certification Training and presents the advanced principles underlying the change process with the specific intent of self-realizing, self-actualizing, and unraveling the conditioning of your past.

The training will take you beyond remedial behavioral changes to the more generative transformation of beliefs and identity. Using advanced “sub-modality” techniques, “timeline reimprinting”, and “sleight of mouth” patterns you will learn how to let go of old, limiting belief systems and replace them with new, empowering ones.

HYPNOSIS MASTER PRACTITIONER

The Master Practitioner of Hypnosis teaches you to master the art and science of hypnotic techniques for creating generative trance in a fun and experiential environment, with hands-on support throughout the training and into your practice as a hypnotherapist.

You will learn a variety of hypnotic techniques like the Ericksonian Model, The Dave Elman Induction as well as hypnotic techniques for dealing with phobias, trauma, anxiety and addictions, weight loss and pain and much more.

Your experiential learning will be supported with an in-depth manual and a library of additional resources.

Followed by a written and practical assessment , you will receive an internationally accredited certification recognised by the American Board of Hypnosis and more importantly you will have the confidence and credentials to practice as a certified Hypnotherapist anywhere in the world.

mBIT (multiple Brain Integration Techniques) is a highly effective approach to achieve greater clarity, strength and peace in all your endeavours; from day-to-day living, to personal development and to be able to ‘shift’ yourself/clients/patients/loved-ones in areas where they may feel stuck, in a quick way.

mBIT is the utilization of mBraining and is informed by the latest neuroscientific discoveries, as well as using methods such as NLP (Neuro Linguistic Programming), cognitive linguistics and behavioural modelling. The new field of mBIT has created a powerful system of communicating with and integrating the wisdom and intelligence of our multiple brains.

This mBIT Coach Certification training is accredited by the International Coach Federation (ICF) to provide CCEUs and Resource Development points.
